Events

Whether you're looking for a night out with your friends, a day of volunteering with your classmates, or some bonding time with your new fellow students, there is an event for everyone! Our wide array of events, gives any, and every student an opportunity to find something that suits them!

Shag Week

Here at TUD, we're very, very passionate about safe sex! We want to make sure that at every turn, our students don't experience any scratches or burns! This year, TUD Kevin Street is hosting the annual "Shag Sexual Awareness" event for the 3rd year running! A sure place to be for young lovebirds!

Table Quiz

There's no better way to connect with your new classmates than over an activity that showcases just how unintelligent they are! Here at TUD, we host a table quiz every month, at every campus! So there's always an opportunity to see just how far Angel's knowledge of curry expands!

1920s Auction

Have you forgotten what auctions even are? In this digital era that we live in, it is very easy to forget how items were bought and sold by our ancestors. At this 1920s-styled auction we aim to recreate what was possible back in the golden age. Cars, Household Appliances, Slaves & Tools along with many other items will be sold.
